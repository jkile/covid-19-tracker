What is the Coronavirus?

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is a respiratory illness that can spread from person to person. The virus causes COVID-19 was first identified during an investigation in an outbreak in Wuhan, China.

How does it spread?

Study’s think that the virus had come from an animal and is now spreading from person to person. The virus is thought to spread mainly between people who are in close contact with one another (within about 6 feet) through respiratory droplets produced when an infected person coughs or sneezes.

​

What are some symptoms?

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breathe

Vaccines/Treatment?

As of now there is no vaccines to protect against the virus and there is no specific antiviral treatments for COVID-19.
